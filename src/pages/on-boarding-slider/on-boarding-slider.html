retail

You’re a store owner with many loyal customers that swear by the products you sell. Your best customers recommend your store to their friends all the time, and gladly do so, without the expectation of rewards or compensation. You have no easy way to empower, track and reward your customers for their referrals to your business.

This is about to change.

Qupey is a mobile app that allows you to track referrals, gain insights and reward your loyal customers with discounts and gifts, of your choosing, for their referrals to your business.
